The Rules

  1. You are X, your friend is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares
  2. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  3. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
